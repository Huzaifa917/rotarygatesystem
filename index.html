Innovation in Gate Automation – Built for Reliability & Durability

Rotary Gate Systems began as a whiteboard idea in the early 2000s, the brainchild of Michael Jones, with input from his employees who had years of experience installing, servicing, and repairing thousands of gate operator systems. With a deep understanding of customer needs, we created durable, high-performance gate operators that solve real-world problems.

Designed to withstand harsh environments, our operators prevent corrosion and ensure long-term performance. Our commitment to quality means that every product is assembled, tested, and shipped from Pensacola, Florida, using components sourced from U.S. manufacturers whenever possible.
